# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a focused, respectful, and technically useful project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Assume good faith, but keep discussions evidence-based.
- Share reproducible examples for bugs and detector behavior.
- Respect maintainers' time when asking for support.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing real secrets, private code, or exploit details in public issues.
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith arguments after maintainers have made a decision.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its users.
